Turtle Quest

Overview

Turtle Quest is a 3D mobile game where you play as a turtle in an interactive underwater environment that has become filled with trash. Your goal is to recycle the cans and plastic boxes to clean up your ocean home (collect and take it to blue recycle bins on ocean floor), while eating jellyfish for speed boosts and avoiding the shark trying to eat you!

This game was made over the course of 2 days for the Global Game Jam 2019, Here is our entry page TurtleQuest

The game was made without using any traditional art assets. The terrain, coral, rocks, and kelp were procedurally generated, the textures were done with shader programs, and everything else was built with Unity primitive shapes.
