Wabbit Slide

Overview

I made this game over the course of a week for a school assignment and later adapted it to work for the video game development club's minigame project (similar to warioware or mario party). Each minigame has a time limit of 30 seconds so the goal of this game is to get your max speed over 100 in under 30 seconds! This web version is a little laggy but overall works pretty well. Lastly, the slide is randomly generated in real time using Perlin noise.

ps: I just realized this actually works on mobiles now... I will be adding touch / tilt support asap!!!
