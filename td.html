Tower Defense Game Instructions

To play this game you must first tune into my stream
Or if we are streaming on discord you can just join my chat

GOAL: prevent enemies from travelling from here to here
Do so by building cool towers with the gold you get!

Here is a list of the game commands:
note: replace XY with desired column letter and row number ( ex: B17 )

!join
join the game, you should see your name appear in the top left with your money count

!leave
leave the game, your towers will remain and your money will be saved if you rejoin

!line XY cost: 50
shoots a single shot at its target

!cone XY cost: 100
shoots 3 shots in a cone at its target

!prism XY cost: 200
links with nearby prisms to form a powerful beam network

!ricochet XY cost: 400
shoots a ball that spazzes out and kills everything

!frost XY cost: 500
shoots an icebolt that explodes on impact, slowing all nearby

!rocket XY cost: 800
shoots a rocket that blows up for big damage

!circle XY cost: 1000
volleys many shots at once in a circle around it

!sniper XY cost: 1000
shoots a single high damage shot at any range. does more damage to further targets.

!machinegun XY cost: 2000
sprays a ton of bullets in a cone at its target

!laser XY cost: 4000
assemble 4 of these in a 2x2 pattern to form the most ultimate damaging tower


!sell XY - sells the tower you own at this spot
