Mike's Fun Adventure

Overview

This is a game I made for my friend Mike for a secret santa present exchange we had on Christmas 2019. It was really fun to work on because I have a lot of silly pictures of him and it was fun to make a challenging game for him to beat. He played it in front of everyone and we all had a good time. Here is a pic of him playing it and having a blast. This is one of those game that's best experienced rather than explained, so I would just dive right in!
